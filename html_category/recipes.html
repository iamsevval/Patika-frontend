Brownie Cookie Recipe

We make wonderful cookies with its texture, flavor.A recipe that everyone will love big and small - these cookies will fill you with chocolate.

Number of people: 8-10 people

Preparation time: 20min Preparation

Cooking time: 15min Cooking

Ingredients For Browni Cookie Recipe

  • 2 Eggs (Room temperature)
  • 1 Cup of sugar missing from 1 finger
  • 1 Cup of flour missing from 1 finger
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter or margarine (You can also use it)
  • 180-200 grams of dark chocolate 3-3.5 packs of â€“ milk can also use)
  • 2 Tablespoons of cocoa
  • 1 packet of vanillin
  • 1/2 (half) package baking powder
  • 1/2 (half) package baking powder
  • 1 flick salt
  • 1 cup walnut (Shredded)
  • 1/2 (half) pack of white chocolate (Fractured)

How is Brownie Cookie Recipe Made?

  1. The eggs are taken into a flick of salt, sugar and vanillin whiskers. The color is whipped until it is opened. You can also do it with a mixer.
  2. Butter and chocolate are melted in a pan. Be careful not to burn. It is transferred into the mortar when it is warm. Other dry materials are also added and mixed. It will be like a dark brown cake.
  3. Then optional pieces of walnut and white chocolate are added. It adds a very nice flavor. You can also use chocolates if you want.
  4. Intermittently placed in the tray with an ice cream spoon or a tablespoon of greasy paper. We don't press on it. They will spread when cooking.
  5. In the preheated oven at the bottom upper setting is cooked at 175 degrees 14-15 min. Do not pass the time will harden. Served when warm or cold.

Note: If you like soft, you can heat and serve a little in the microwave oven when it cools down.
